KFC (also known as Kentucky Fried Chicken) is an American fast food restaurant chain headquartered in Louisville, Kentucky that specializes in fried chicken. It is the world's second-largest restaurant chain (as measured by sales) after McDonald's, with 22,621 locations globally in > 150 countries as of December 2019.[5] The chain is a subsidiary of Yum! Brands, a rest aurant company that also owns the Pizza Hut, Taco Bell & WingStreet chains.[6]
